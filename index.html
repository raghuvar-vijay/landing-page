FAQs

Answers to questions you might have about Lira.

What does Lira do? +
Lira is an accessible exam creation platform that helps educators create inclusive assessments for students with diverse abilities. It automatically converts traditional exams into accessible formats and provides customized assistance based on individual student needs.
Who is Lira for? +
Lira is designed for educators, schools, and educational institutions who want to create inclusive learning environments. It's perfect for teachers who need to accommodate students with visual, auditory, motor, or cognitive disabilities.
How secure is my data on Lira? +
We take data security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, we comply with educational privacy standards like FERPA, and we never share your data with third parties. Your exam content and student information remain completely private and secure.
Is Lira easy to use if you're not technical? +
Absolutely! Lira is designed with educators in mind, not tech experts. Our intuitive interface requires no special training - simply upload your existing exams, and Lira handles the accessibility conversion automatically. Our step-by-step guidance makes it easy for anyone to create inclusive assessments.
